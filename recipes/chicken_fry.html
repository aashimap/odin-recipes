CHICKEN FRY

This chicken fry is so delicious. We use special ingredients to make it crispy.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken - 1 kg
  • Lemon - 2
  • Ginger-Garlic paste - 2 tsp
  • Chilli powder - 2 tbsp
  • Rice powder - 3 tsp
  • Corn flour - 2 tsp
  • Salt - as required

STEPS

  1. Cut and wash chicken
  2. Marinate with the other ingredients
  3. Rest for 20 mins
  4. Deep fry until golden brown
